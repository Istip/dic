THE PARTNERS

Design Integrated Construct (DIC) is a design-build firm delivering breakthrough solutions, seamless integration and impeccable implementation of highly complex projects in mission critical, renewable energy and infrastructure.

The three managing partners worked together for over ten years, combining complementary engineering expertise and sharing a passion for cutting edge projects. In 2012, they co-founded DIC with a mission to provide their clients with umparalleled capabilities, pushing the boundary of project design and delivery to new levels.

Since its inception, the team worked with unmatched commitment on landmark projects setting the standards for high-tech project execution in the region.

Today, DIC stands as the partner of choice for clients looking for end to end innovative services that optimize performance, mitigate risks, mazimize energy efficiency and bring projects to full fruition on time and on budget.
